Runs the conformance test families from docs/conformance-spec.md against the real runTurn pipeline, in your browser, with the same src/ modules the app loads. Deterministic — echo model + hash embedder, no network, no weights. That determinism is also the ceiling: it measures the answerable void verdict and the veto flags, but it cannot measure FM2 (confabulation at a void) — that needs a live generative model. Results are a baseline-run artifact, not a pass/fail certificate.
