    how a text becomes music — one loop, no theory imposed

    This is a self-contained output organ. Where organs/in/music.js reads a melody and the reader recovers a tonic from γ-mass, The Seam reverses the arrow: it reads a text onto the same spine and renders the reading. The mapping is mostly automatic, because the engine's three dynamics already are the three pillars of music cognition.

    the one loop — prediction → strain → seam → holon

    Prediction projects an expectation (Meyer · Narmour · Huron · IDyOM). The gap between expectation and event is surprise — information content, the reader's own −log p / D_KL channel. That surprise is felt as tension, which also grows with distance from the tonic (Lerdahl's Tonal Pitch Space · Larson's musical forces). When tension accumulates and discharges, that is a seam; where surprise spikes, a boundary forms (Pearce & Wiggins). The nested spectrum of those boundaries is the emergent form (GTTM grouping · Lerdahl's tension tree). Reading runs this loop over a text; music runs it over tones. Same loop, two streams.

    the operator algebra, sounded

    INS — a figure appearsa leitmotif stated; first appearance is the full motif, later appearances are fragments, varied by all that has happened since (the γ-decayed integral name)
    CON — two figures relatecounterpoint between two stated themes — and never before both are stated (the arity gate is literally true)
    γ-mass rankpitch: the heaviest figure is the tonic, the second the dominant — the very thing the reader extracts from a melody, run backwards
    surprise (IC)dynamics & accent, and the cue for a boundary
    straintonal tension: chords move off the tonic and the suspension is held open
    seam / RECcadence; magnitude sets its strength — section, phrase, or motivic articulation
    entropybrightness & harmonic openness — uncertainty as timbral colour
    void / unresolved figurea held dissonance that colours the section until it resolves
    affect / valencemode · key · tempo — the colour, the only thing read from feeling rather than structure

    So affect sets the palette and structure sets the building — the same content-vs-shape split the reader runs (completing what TransProse began). Leitmotif is the historical proof a referent's accumulated identity can live in sound; sonata form is the proof the dependency order already governs music. The structure is the book's; the player only measures it and lets you hear it.

    Sound is General-MIDI samples when HQ is on (loaded on demand), and a built-in Web-Audio synthesis engine otherwise — so it always runs, offline.
